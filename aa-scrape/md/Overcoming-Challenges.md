#### Activity Title
Overcoming Challenges
#### Activity Type
Class Meeting
#### Objective
Identify challenges to achieving goals and how to overcome them

#### Preparation
1. If possible, seat the class in a circle.
2. Sit where you can participate comfortably in the discussion.

#### Instructions Header
Class Meeting Instructions
#### Warm-Up
Have everyone in the class (including you!) briefly answer the following question:

**What is a big goal you have?**<br/> 
              (Get an A in English. Get on the varsity basketball team. Star on a TV show.)
#### Discussion
As a class, discuss one or more of the following prompts, as time allows:


-  **What types of daily activities do you do that could help you achieve your goals?**

-  **What are some daily challenges that could make achieving your goals difficult?**

-  **What could you do to overcome those challenges?**

-  **What could other people do to help you achieve your goals?**
#### Reflection
Have the class reflect quietly about the following prompt:

**Think of a goal you have. What is one thing you will try to do every day to help you achieve your goal? **

Call on students to tell the class their ideas, as time allows.
